About Me

I am a final year Bachelor student of software engineering at the University of Guilan. I have three years of experience in programming and software and website development. My specialty is Java programming language and Spring framework. I have experience in developing many academic and commercial projects. I was fascinated by the concept of artificial intelligence before entering university (from the first out of thousands of times that I saw the terminator series). After surfing the internet, inquiring from specialists, and consulting with professors and senior students, I became acquainted with the machine learning and began to learn it.
